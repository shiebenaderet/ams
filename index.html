🧭 Welcome Start of Year Introductions, expectations, and getting ready for the year ahead. Unit 1 13 Colonies Explore the founding and growth of the original thirteen British colonies in North America. Unit 2 The Triangle Trade Examine the transatlantic trade routes that shaped economies and lives across three continents. Unit 3 Revolutionary Voices Hear from the people who ignited a revolution and fought for independence. Unit 4 The Constitution Dive into the document that created a new form of government and still guides us today. Unit 5 Semester Capstone Bring together everything from the first semester in a culminating project. Unit 6 SCOTUS Study landmark Supreme Court cases and their lasting impact on American life. Unit 7 The Early Republic Follow the young nation as it tests its new government and faces early challenges. Unit 8 Westward Expansion Trace the push westward and its consequences for all peoples of the continent. Unit 9 The Civil War Understand the causes, course, and consequences of the war that redefined the nation. Unit 10 Semester 2 Capstone Reflect on the full year and showcase your learning in a final capstone project.
